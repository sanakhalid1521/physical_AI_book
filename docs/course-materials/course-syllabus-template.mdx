## Course Description
This course provides a comprehensive introduction to Physical AI and humanoid robotics, focusing on the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems. Students will learn fundamental concepts, implement practical applications, and explore the challenges of embodied intelligence.

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, students will be able to:
1. Understand the fundamental principles of Physical AI
2. Implement basic perception and control systems for humanoid robots
3. Use simulation environments for robot development and testing
4. Apply AI algorithms to physical systems with real-time constraints
5. Analyze the ethical and safety considerations in humanoid robotics
